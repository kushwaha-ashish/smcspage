Admissions Application (2023-2024)

Thank you for your interest in St. Matthew Catholic School.

Interested families may download and print an application from the links below, or contact the school at info@smcshillsboro.org to have a registration mailed. Completed applications can either be emailed, mailed or dropped off at the school office. Mailed applications should be sent to: St. Matthew Catholic School, 221 SE Walnut Street, Hillsboro, OR 97123.

Application along with non-refundable application fee of $35 (K-8th) or $75 (preschool) per student should be enclose with application and all required documents to ensure timely processing. Documents include a copy of the applicant's birth certificate, and baptismal certificate (if they have been baptized). Students applying to transfer from another school should also include a copy of their most recent report card.
