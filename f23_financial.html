Financial Aid

In order to be considered for financial assistance, you MUST fill out the FACTS application online and have applied and been accepted at St. Matthew. The deadline to apply for the 2022-2023 school year is March 1st 2022.

The Archdiocese of Portland determines a school's need for assistance based on qualified applicants applying through FACTS (link below).
